## v1.0.0 - Initial Simplified Release - [2026-02-03]

### `Overview`

AMRfinderflow is a streamlined fork of nf-core/funcscan v3.0.0, focused exclusively on antibiotic resistance gene (ARG) detection. This pipeline retains the core quality control, annotation, and screening functionality while removing AMP screening, BGC screening, and MultiQC reporting.
